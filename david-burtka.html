David Burtka is an American actor, writer, singer, and professional chef best known for his guest role in CSI: NY and for the role of ‘Scooter’ in How I Met Your Mother. He has also won the Clarence Derwent Award for the most promising male performer for his performance as ‘Boy’ in “The Play About the Baby” by Edward Albee. He has a huge social media fan base with more than 500k followers on Instagram and more than 200k followers on Twitter.

Education

David graduated from Salem High School in 1993. Burtka obtained his Bachelor of Fine Arts from the University of Michigan. He graduated from Le Cordon Bleu College of Culinary Arts in Pasadena in 2009.

David trained as an actor at the Interlochen Center for the Arts in Northwest Michigan. He also trained in acting at the William Esper Studios in Manhattan, New York.
