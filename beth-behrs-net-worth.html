Major Sources of Beth Behrs’ Income

Television Roles

Beth Behrs has multiple streams of income that contribute to her impressive net worth. First and foremost, her acting career has been a significant source.

She starred in the hit CBS comedy 2 Broke Girls, earning a substantial salary per episode. After the series ended, she landed another main role in the CBS sitcom The Neighborhood.

Film Appearances

In addition to her television roles, Behrs has appeared in several films. These include the black comedy Adventures of Serial Buddies and the romantic dramedy Hello, My Name is Doris. Her role in the Pixar animated film Monsters University also added to her income.
