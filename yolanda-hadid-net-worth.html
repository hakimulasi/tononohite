Yolanda Hadid Wiki Biography

Yolanda Hadid Foster, commonly known as Yolanda Hadid, is a famous American model, actress, interior designer, as well as a reality television star. To the public, Yolanda Hadid is perhaps best known as a member of the reality television series called “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills”, which airs on Bravo network. Hadid joined Kim Richards, Kyle Richards, Lisa Vanderpump, and Brandi Glanville in the third season of the show, and has been present on the series ever since. “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” serves as the sixth installment in “The Real Housewives” series, among “The Real Housewives of New York City”, “New Jersey”, “Atlanta” and “D.C.”. Currently, the series airs its fifth season. Considered to be among the highest-rated television series with approximately 2.42 million viewers per episode, “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” received a Critics Choice Award in 2011 for “Best Reality Series”.

Yolanda Hadid Net Worth $45 Million

A well-known reality television personality, how rich is Yolanda Hadid? According to sources, in 2003 she received as much as $30,000 in child support every month, and that same year earned $3.6 million. In 2013, her salary per episode of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills” amounted to $100,000. In regards to her overall wealth, Yolanda Hadid’s net worth is estimated to be $45 million, most of which she has accumulated due to her appearances on television screens. Among Hadid’s most valuable assets is her house in Malibu, which is valued at $6 million.

Yolanda Hadid was born in 1964 in the Netherlands, however she currently resides in Malibu, California. Hadid started her career by appearing as a runway model for the fashion designer Frans Molenaar. Eventually, she was noticed by a co-founder of the modeling agency called “Ford Models” Eileen Ford. Impressed by Hadid’s looks and skills, Ford offered her an opportunity to work for her company. Eventually, Yolanda Hadid signed a modeling deal with “Ford Models”, as a result of which she has travelled to and worked in such cities as Milan, Tokyo, Los Angeles and New York. At the time, Hadid was able to financially support herself through her modeling career.

Hadid stayed in the fashion industry for over 15 years, during which she made a name for herself. However, wishing to settle down in one place instead of traveling the world, Hadid decided to end her modeling career. In 2012, Yolanda Hadid joined the cast of “The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills”, thus launching a career in the television industry. Aside from being a model and a reality television star, Hadid is an owner of a business called “Hopelessly Romantic”, which focuses on helping couples maintain healthy relationships.

In regards to her personal life, Yolanda Hadid has been married twice. Firstly, she celebrated her marriage to Mohamed Hadid in 1994, with whom she lived until 2000. The couple have three children together, namely Anwar Hadid, Bella Hadid and Gigi Hadid, who followed her mother’s footsteps and became a fashion model. In 2010, Hadid became engaged to the musician and record producer David Foster, whom she married a year later.
