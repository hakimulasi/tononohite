From Vietnam Veteran to Reality TV Sensation

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Si Robertson, also known as Uncle Si, is an American TV personality and former military. Robertson is famous for starring in the A&E's reality series Duck Dynasty along with his family members. Likewise, he has appeared on TV shows like Buck Commander and The Duckmen of Louisiana.

Si was born Silas Merritt Robertson on April 27, 1948, in Vivian, Louisiana, USA. He is the sixth of seven children of James Harold Robertson (father) and Merritt Hale Robertson (mother). Also, Uncle Si graduated from North Caddo High School in his hometown.

Si Robertson 'Uncle Si' Was The Victim Of A Death Hoax

In March 2020, A&E's Duck Dynasty's leading cast member Si Roberston's dead hoax circulated all over the internet.

The news regarding Uncle Si's death is unreal. Si Robertson himself announced that his death rumor is fake via social media posts.

In a Facebook post, Si wrote:

Hey Jack! There is a rumor going around that I have passed away. I’m still alive and kickin y’all! Don’t believe everything said on the internet, just like my stories it’s usually only 95% true!

Si Robertson Is Married To His Wife, Christine Raney Robertson

The Vietnam vet, Si is in a marital relationship with his beloved wife, Christine Raney Robertson. Christine and Si tied the wedding knot back in 1971 when Si was in his early twenties.

Since that day, the husband-wife duo has been sharing a good bond as a happily married pair. Si and his wife Christine are now blissfully celebrating their 50th marriage anniversary without being involved in any rumors and controversies related to their separation. 

Christine & Sil shared two adorable kids Trasa Robertson (daughter) and Scott Merritt Robertson (son), from their marital relationship. Likewise, Si and his spouse Christine are also the proud grandparents of their eight grandchildren.

Relation With Phil Robertson

The 5-feet-10-inches tall Si and Phil Robertson are brothers. Phil Alexander Robertson is a professional hunter, businessman, and cast member of the Duck Dynasty.

Phil is highly recognized as the founder of the multi-million company Duck Commander. The company is a brand of a duck call. It also manufactures buck calls under the Buck Commander brand.

There in the company, Uncle Si also served as a Duck-call maker. Phil has been married to Marsha Kay Carroway since 1966. Marsha and Phil are the blessed parents of Jase, Phillips, Jep, Alan, and Willie Robertson. They are also featured on the Duck Dynasty show.

Moreover, Willie's children Rebecca, John Luke, Sadie Robertson, Bella, Will, and Rowdy Robertson have also been featured. Furthermore, besides Phil, Uncle Si has five other siblings named Jimmy Frank, Harold, Tommy, Judy, and Jan Robertson.

Net Worth And Earnings

Si Robertson has a net worth of $8 million, which he collected from his professional work. Si is a TV personality who rose to prominence after he appeared in the series Duck Dynasty. 

Besides these, he has also starred the shows like Last Man Standing, Faith of Our Fathers, and Going Si-Ral.
