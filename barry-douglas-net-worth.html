He has performed with major orchestras such as the London Symphony Orchestra, the Russian National Orchestra, and the Vancouver Symphony Orchestra. He is known for his performances at venues worldwide, including the Verbier Festival and the BBC Proms.

Yes, he was awarded the Order of the British Empire (OBE) in 2002 for his services to music.
