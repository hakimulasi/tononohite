Garik Martirosyan Full Overview and Wiki

A Journey Through Entertainment

His story is not just a tale of laughter; it’s a saga of transformation and triumph.

Born in Yerevan, Armenia, to Yuriy and Zhasmin Martirosyan, Garik’s early life was far removed from the glitz of showbiz.

His academic pursuits led him to a degree in Neurology from the Yerevan State Medical University, but the call of the stage proved too strong to resist.

From Medicine to Comedy

His foray into entertainment began with the KVN (Club of the Funny and Inventive), where he not only participated but shone as the captain of the New Armenians team.

His natural charisma and wit quickly caught the public’s eye, propelling him from the medical halls to the center stage of Russian comedy.

The Comedy Club and Beyond

In 2003, alongside fellow New Armenians, Garik founded the Comedy Club, a venture that would revolutionize Russian comedy.

Under his co-production, the show became a staple on the TNT network, blending sketch and stand-up comedy to the delight of millions. But Garik’s talents weren’t confined to laughter alone.

A Glimpse into the Political Arena

His journey took an unexpected turn in 2007 when he dipped his toes into politics, contributing to the establishment of the United Liberal-National Party in Armenia.

Though primarily known for his entertainment prowess, this move highlighted his deep connection to his roots and a desire to impact Armenian society positively.

A Personal Touch

On the personal front, his life is as rich and fulfilling as his career. Married to Zhanna Martirosyan, with two children, Jasmine and Daniel, he balances the demands of his public persona with his role as a family man.

FAQs about Garik Martirosyan

When was Garik Martirosyan born?

What is Garik Martirosyan’s nationality?

He is of Armenian nationality and is a Russia-based entertainer.

What professions is he known for?

He is recognized as a comedian, showman, television presenter, stand-up comedian, actor, neurologist, and psychotherapist.

What educational background does he have?

He graduated from Yerevan State Medical University with a degree in neurology in 2002 and worked three years in a psychological hospital.

Is Garik Martirosyan married?

How did he start his career in entertainment?

His entertainment career began after joining the New Armenians team of the Russian TV show KVN from 1993 to 2002, where he served as captain from 1997. In 2003, with the help of fellow New Armenians, he founded Comedy Club.
