Meet Caitlyn Jenner's Six Biological Kids!

August 2024 · 5 minute read

Caitlyn Jenner has been a famous beacon for the LGBTQ+ community worldwide since 2015. Who are Caitlyn Jenner’s kids, and how many are they? 

The intriguing lives of Caitlyn Jenner’s six biological children are a testament to the intertwining of family, identity, and shared experiences that have shaped their journeys alongside their famous mother. Here’s a look at Caitlyn Jenner’s kids’ lives.

Who Is Caitlyn Jenner?

Caitlyn Marie Jenner (born William Bruce Jenner; formerly known as Bruce Jenner until 2015) is an American media personality and former Olympic gold medal-winning decathlete. She was born on October 28, 1949, in Mount Kisco, New York, to parents Esther Ruth (née McGuire) and William Hugh Jenner. 

In her younger years, Caitlyn attended Graceland College (now Graceland University) in Lamoni, Iowa, and earned a football scholarship. Sadly, she was forced to stop playing football because of a knee injury. The Graceland track coach L. D. Weldon encouraged her to switch to the decathlon as he believed she had the potential for success. 

Caitlyn debuted as a decathlete in 1970 in the Drake Relays decathlon in Des Moines, Iowa, finishing in fifth place. Jenner graduated from Graceland College in 1973 with a degree in physical education.

She went on to win a couple of big awards for her country and broke some world records making her a decorated Olympic athlete. It is important to note that Caitlyn’s Olympic events were men’s events that occurred before her gender transition.

In a 20/20 television interview in April 2015, Caitlyn came out as a trans woman explaining how she had dealt with gender dysphoria since she was young. She underwent cosmetic surgery and completed sex reassignment surgery in January 2017.

In September 2015, her name was legally changed to ‘Caitlyn Marie Jenner,’ and her gender to female.

Meet Caitlyn Jenner’s Six Kids

Caitlyn has six biological children with three different women. They are Burton, Cassandra, Brandon, Brody, Kendall, and Kylie Jenner. Here is everything we know about all six children in chronological order. 

Burton ‘Burt’ Jenner

Burton William Jenner is the eldest of Caitlyn Jenner’s six children. He was born on September 6, 1978, in Los Angeles to Caitlyn and her then-wife Chrystie Crownover and was named after Caitlyn’s late younger brother, who died in a car crash in 1976. 

Burt is a father of three boys with his longtime girlfriend and mostly keeps out of the spotlight. He is also a professional truck racer and has loved racing since his dad took him go-karting as a child. Caitlyn had a strained relationship with her eldest son after her third marriage, but the two have reconnected and have been on good terms since her transition in 2015. 

Cassandra ‘Casey’ Jenner

Caitlyn and Chrystie’s second child, Cassandra Jenner (now Cassandra Marino), was born on June 10, 1980, when her parents were in the middle of a divorce, and Caitlyn was not present for the birth in L.A. 

Cassandra is now an interior designer and married to her husband, Micheal Marino. She has three children and keeps her life very private. She also had a strained relationship with Caitlyn in her early years, but the two have since reconnected and are working on improving their relationship. 

Brandon Jenner

Brandon is Caitlyn’s third child but first with her second wife, Linda Thompson, an actress and songwriter. He was born in June 1981 and is one of the most publicly known children of the Olympian. Brandon is a singer and songwriter with over a million followers on Instagram, sharing updates on his new music with his fans. 

He has been married twice and has three children. Like Burt and Cassandra, Brandon has described his relationship with Caitlyn as once strained. He has also been reported to be mending his relationship with Caitlyn in recent years. 

Brody Jenner

Brody is Caitlyn and Linda’s second child and her fourth biological child. He was born on August 21, 1983, in L.A. Known to be very fond of cameras, he has been in several reality shows over the years, such as “The Princes of Malibu,” “Bromance,” “Sex with Brody,” “Kourtney and Kim Take New York,” and “Very Cavallari” to name a few. He also has his line of hard Seltzer called Mamitas.

The reality star was married and divorced in 2019 but has been reportedly dating again. Brody reportedly has a good relationship with his mom and siblings and is rebuilding his relationship with Caitlyn. 

Kendall Jenner

Caitlyn and Linda divorced in 1986, and she married Kris Jenner (née Kardashian) in 1991. The pair stayed together until Caitlyn transitioned in 2015 and had two children, Kendall and Kylie. Kendall Nicole Jenner was born on November 3, 1995, in L.A. 

Kendall has become a fashion icon and a reality star from her appearances on the popular “Keeping Up With the Kardashians.” She is now one of the world’s most popular and most-paid supermodels. Kendall has always described herself as a “daddy’s girl,” having a great relationship with Caitlyn from birth. 

Kylie Jenner

Probably the most popular of the Jenner children, Kylie Jenner was born on August 10, 1997, in L.A. She has also appeared in many reality series like KUWTK and her spin-off series “Life of Kylie.” Besides reality shows, Kylie has been very business savvy, launching several brands, such as the world-famous makeup brand “Kylie Cosmetics.” 

She became the youngest self-made billionaire in March 2019. Kylie is also reported to have a great relationship with Caitlyn, and the two are regularly seen out in public together. 

Caitlyn also has four stepchildren from her third wife, Kris. They are Kourtney, Kim, Khloe, and Rob Kardashian. Fun fact: Caitlyn has over 22 grandchildren in total!
