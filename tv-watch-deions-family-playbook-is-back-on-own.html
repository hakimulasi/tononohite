TV WATCH: DEIONS FAMILY PLAYBOOK IS BACK ON OWN

July 2024 · 1 minute read

Round two has begun. The second season of ‘Deion’s Family Playbook’ is in full swing and the road is getting messy.

While Deion has full control of the boys that he coaches for his school and charity, his kids are pushing the envelope at home. One of his sons decides to buy an exotic car without telling his father, and all of the Sanders kids are getting a bit relaxed in terms of chores and housekeeping. “It’s getting on my nerves,” says Deion.

It was during the first season of ‘Family Playbook’ that we watched Deion receive counsel from Snoop Dogg. Sanders was telling the rapper about his older son, Shilo, who had no desire to follow in his father’s footsteps at the time. “You know it hurts you as a father that they’re not going down the path that you want,” said Deion. Snoop Dogg gave him words of comfort and advised the former NFL player to “let [Shilo] walk his own path. You’ve got to let him go into uncharted territory.”

The kids are definitely going into uncharted territory this season with wild spending habits and unkempt rooms. Check out a snippet from the show below. You can also catch the latest episode of ‘Deion’s Family Playbook’ on OWN this Saturday.
