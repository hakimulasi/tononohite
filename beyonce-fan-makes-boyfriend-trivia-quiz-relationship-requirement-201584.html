Beyonce Fan Makes BF Take Trivia Quiz as Relationship Requirement

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Longterm boyfriend or bug-a-boo? That was the question obsessive Beyonce “stan” Allie Davis (@Fergyonce) was trying to answer on Monday, April 6, when she quizzed her boyfriend on the most important questions in life: detailed Beyonce trivia. 

“I gave my boyfriend a Beyoncé exam to make sure we can stay together. He passed,” she tweeted along with pictures of the questions and his answers.

Like most tests, the questions were split up into different part-itions, and the future Jay Z to Davis’ Beyonce had to score a 60 percent or higher to stay in their relationship. 

It started off simple, asking the beau to name three Beyonce songs, and got more detailed when one question asked, “Briefly explain the Solange Knowles elevator incident. Who was involved? What was the public’s reaction? Did Beyonce respond to the incident?”

???? pic.twitter.com/zgl1YbwSDe

Davis’ boyfriend wrote, “a billion dollars and Jay Z got punched.” 

The Beyhive member peppered in some questions about her other favorite artists including One Direction, Jay Z, and Nicki Minaj. But the majority of the points were reserved for Queen Bey. Luckily Davis’ man scored an 80 percent, proving their futures will be filled with drinking watermelon and riding surfboards. 

Soon after she posted the test, it started getting picked up all over the Internet. Davis noted that some readers even got aggressive.

“I swear some of y'all need to find your chill and maybe a sense of humor…sending death threats over a Beyoncé joke? Pls,” she tweeted.

Some critics bashed her for leaving her relationship up to chance, but Davis replied, “The Beyoncé test literally took 5 minutes to write and I did it because I was bored and I like to mess with my boyfriend… PSA from my boyfriend: ‘I knew this was a joke from the beginning, and sometimes we love to pester each other.’” 

After the news went viral, Davis posted a “create your own” Beyonce exam on her blog, and later made a detailed mock resume, writing, “Beyoncé, here is my resume, I am applying for Beygency President.”
