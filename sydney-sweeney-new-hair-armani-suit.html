    The fashion set recently went through an unexpected blonde renaissance, including Bella Hadid, Jenna Colman and Kim Kardashian.

    But as ever, Sydney Sweeney is ahead of the curve. The superstar is over her platinum phase, despite her admitting that she got offered more roles with blonde tresses.

    Sydney wore a rhinestone suit from Mcqueen

    MORE: Sydney Sweeney poses in nothing but jewellery for her Tory Burch campaign

    She has a habit of stealing the limelight wherever she appears thanks to her innate ability to shine on the red carpet which is perhaps why she was the poster girl for Miu Miu and Tory Burch last year.  And this time she combined the glamour with her stunning new golden brown locks, wearing the most daring yet dazzling suit we’ve possibly ever seen. 

    The star attended the Berlinale film festival in Germany and brought sophisticated drama with her look courtesy of her trusty stylist Molly Dickson.

    @sydneysweeney

    MORE: Sydney Sweeney's stylist reveals exactly how they created her custom Oscar De La Renta gown for the Emmys

    She wore an Alexander Mcqueen suit with cut outs so risque that a show of model-approved underboob became part of the aesthetic. The jacket boasted diamond-encrusting that draped from front to back, finished off with jewelled tassels that created a slightly oversized silhouette.

    She showed off her locks loose for the  premiere the same evening

    Hair Stylist Glen Coco Oropeza let the suit do the talking, creating a super sleek bun with two strands framing her face, whilst make up artist Melissa Hernandez creating a soft glow using all beauty products.

    Thanks to her skyrocketing fame from Euphoria, the 25-year-old and her platinum blonde tresses have become synonymous in the same way as Pamela Anderson and Marylin Monroe.

    RELATED: Sydney Sweeney wows as the latest poster-girl for Miu Miu's micro mini at the MTV Movie & TV Awards

    But she told earlier this month “I’m naturally a brunette — almost all of my life I was a brunette.”

    She continued “I highlighted my hair for Everything Sucks, but I really went blonde for Euphoria. There was this complex where no one really knew me as brunette Syd, and I didn’t really book as much as I did when I was blonde”

    “I’m back to brunette because the blonde was killing my hair, and I needed to grow it out a little bit. I kind of feel like I’m myself again.”
